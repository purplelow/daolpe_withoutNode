A CUSTOMIZED SOLUTIONS PROVIDER We tailor our investments to meet the needs of selling shareholders, founders / family owners and management teams and we often navigate complex situations to generate a robust pipeline of proprietary investment opportunities.

HIGHLY EXPERIENCED BUYOUT SPECIALISTS We are one team and together we are better than any one of us alone. DAOL Private Equity Limited team has decades of experience and track record of buying right and making businesses better across multiple economic and business cycles, geographies and industries.

A LEADING MIDDLE MARKET PRIVATE EQUITY INVESTOR DAOL Private Equity Limited is an experienced middle-market buyout specialist firm investing in Korea-related investment opportunities at home and abroad. Our flagship funds invest across a diverse array of sectors where we have been most successful, including general industrial & manufacturing, branded / non-branded consumer & retail and business services.

COMMITTED TO BUILDING LONG-TERM VALUE Beyond financial resources, DAOL Private Equity Limited provides strategic and operational support to its portfolio companies for their long-term success. DPEL investment professionals and operating partners work seamlessly with best-in-class entrepreneurs and proven management teams to structurally enhance the strategic position, competitiveness, and profitability of underlying companies, providing a solid basis for continuity, growth and prosperity.
